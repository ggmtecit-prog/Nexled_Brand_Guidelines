Imagery

Imagery should make NexLed products feel real, precise, and professionally built, with the product always staying at the center.

Visual Direction

Style & Technical Approach

  • •

    Use sharp, realistic imagery that shows materials, construction, and product detail clearly.

  • •

    Favor clean lighting, neutral backgrounds, and controlled environments that keep attention on the product.

  • •

    Prefer frontal, lateral, and macro views that explain form, finish, and intended application.

  • •

    Avoid human models or lifestyle staging that distracts from technical clarity.

Macro & Texture

Close views that reveal finish quality, construction detail, and manufacturing precision.

Product in Context

Minimal, credible environments that explain how the product lives in a real installation.

Technical Perspective

Angles that show geometry, scale, and function without over-staging the composition.

Avoid

Common Imagery Mistakes

Busy backgrounds Harsh shadows Heavy filters People as the focal point Over-styled scenes
